Our Beloved Mascot

Deiphenus, our beloved mascot, is no ordinary pigeon—he’s Fontaine’s finest feathered baker. Perched proudly in a crisp chef’s hat and carrying his trusty hammer, Deiphenus represents the heart and soul of The Breadsmith: hardworking, creative, and a little quirky. Legend has it he learned to bake by watching the finest patissiers from the rooftops of Fontaine, eventually swooping down to join the craft himself. Today, he inspires our team to be bold in the kitchen, precise in technique, and always passionate about the joy a single loaf can bring.

The Story Behind Us

The Breadsmith is a humble yet passionate bakery nestled in the heart of Fontaine, where tradition meets innovation and every loaf tells a story. Founded with the dream of making Fontaine the bread-making capital of Teyvat, The Breadsmith crafts each creation with the finest ingredients, timeless techniques, and a sprinkle of culinary curiosity. From crusty baguettes to airy brioche, we believe great bread brings warmth to every home, connects people, and turns the simplest moments into something special.
